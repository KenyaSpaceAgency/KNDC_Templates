Kenya National Data Cube

Geo-Information for Sustainable Development

The Kenya National Data Cube (KNDC) is an innovative geospatial data platform developed to provide ready-to-use satellite imagery and Earth observation datasets for a wide range of applications. Spearheaded by the Kenya Space Agency and key partners, the KNDC is designed to support government agencies, researchers, developers, and decision-makers in tackling pressing environmental, agricultural, and urban development challenges.

Built on a foundation of open-source tools and cloud-based infrastructure, the KNDC enables efficient access, analysis, and visualization of large volumes of Earth observation data. This approach fosters transparency, data-driven decision-making, and collaborative innovation in addressing national priorities such as food security, climate resilience, and disaster risk management.

The Kenya National Data Cube exemplifies how technology and open data can be leveraged to drive inclusive and sustainable development in Africa and beyond.
